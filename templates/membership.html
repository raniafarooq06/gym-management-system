Membership Plans

At Rania's Gym, we offer flexible membership options to suit your lifestyle and fitness goals. Whether you're looking for a short-term commitment or a long-term fitness journey, we have the perfect plan for you.

Monthly Plan: Ideal for beginners and short-term visitors. Full access to gym equipment, group classes, and personal training sessions available at extra cost.

Quarterly Plan: Best for regular gym-goers. Includes full access, 2 free personal training sessions per month, and free access to group fitness classes.

Annual Plan: Our best value! Includes everything in the quarterly plan plus free nutrition consultations, priority access to new programs, and exclusive gym merchandise.
